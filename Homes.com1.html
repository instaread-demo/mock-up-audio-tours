About This Home

Back on the market and at an incredible price for mid century, South Palm Springs home on fee land! Buyer was unable to perform and price adjusted to allow Sellers to move forward with their plans! This cozy 40 unit complex set up in pinwheel triplex clusters, minimizing shared walls and maximizing privacy. This rare gem offers the timeless charm of the 1960s, masterfully updated for today’s lifestyle. With clean lines and an open floor plan, this home radiates effortless sophistication. This unit is one of approximately 4 in community that allows your outdoor living area to be completely private! Pool area just steps outside your gate, for when you are wanting to be social. The spacious bedrooms serve as tranquil retreats, while the updated bathrooms exude spa-like luxury. There is a large wall free flex space, easily converted to a 3rd Bedroom if needed or ideal for formal dining or casual meeting area. Upgrades include; windows and sliders in November of 2025, new ac in 2024, new paint throughout in October 2025, and renovated floors in kitchen, showers, tub, sinks, suite and laundry, patio lighting, ceilings smoothed and finished in 2021. Step outside to your private courtyard oasis complete with Lemon, Palm Trees and blooming hibiscus -exit rear courtyard door where you’ll discover a sparkling pool surrounded by lush landscaping and breathtaking mountain views. Great location within community with 2 guest parking spaces adjacent to unit and dedicated carport space and storage, right outside your kitchen door.
