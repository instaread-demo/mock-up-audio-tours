Overview

Written by automotive expert Michael Cantu ⓘ
Updated: 7/8/2026

If you're shopping for a budget-friendly small sedan, the 2025 Nissan Versa deserves a look. This subcompact delivers an agreeable driving experience along with fuel economy reaching up to 35 mpg combined. Straight-line performance isn't a highlight, but the Versa compensates with plenty of useful technology and driver assist features for the price, plus a cabin that's surprisingly roomy for the class. For 2025 the car largely carries over, now adding standard LED headlights across all trims and two years of complimentary maintenance.
