About Presidential Towers

Presidential Towers is a landmark residential community in the heart of downtown Chicago, set across four connected high-rise towers on West Madison Street. With direct access to the West Loop, the Loop and major transit, residents are steps from dining, nightlife, shopping and the city's business district.

Apartment homes feature open layouts, modern kitchens with stainless-steel appliances, generous closet space and skyline or city views. The community offers a resort-style amenity package designed for comfort and convenience, with an on-site fitness center, resident lounges, and around-the-clock services.
